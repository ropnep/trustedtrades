About Trusted Tradies Hub

Your trusted partner in connecting with Perth's most reliable trade professionals

Your Local Perth Trade Directory

Trusted Tradies Hub was born from frustration with finding reliable tradespeople in Perth. After experiencing overpriced quotes and unreliable service from multiple tradies, we decided to create a platform that genuinely helps Perth residents connect with verified, trustworthy local professionals.

We understand the stress of needing urgent repairs or quality renovations. That's why we've built a directory that focuses on quality over quantity, featuring only tradies who meet our strict verification standards and have proven track records in the Perth market.

What Makes Us Different

  • Local Perth Focus: We exclusively serve the Perth metropolitan area, ensuring all tradies understand local building codes, weather conditions, and council requirements.
  • License Verification: Every tradesperson undergoes verification of licenses and business credentials through WA Building and Energy before being listed.
  • Genuine Reviews: All ratings and reviews come from verified customers who have actually used these services, not fake or paid reviews.
  • Transparent Pricing: Our directory is completely free for customers. Tradies pay fair annual listing fees, not per-lead charges that inflate your quotes.
  • Personal Recommendations: We highlight tradies we've personally used and trust, sharing our real experiences to help you make informed decisions.

Our Commitment

We're committed to raising the standard of trade services in Perth by promoting professionalism, reliability, and fair pricing. When you use our directory, you're supporting local businesses that value quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To eliminate the stress and uncertainty of finding quality tradespeople in Perth by providing a reliable, transparent platform that benefits both customers seeking excellent service and skilled tradies who take pride in their work.
